Admission Overview

Admissions for the 2026-27 session at RA Bhageria Educational Academy are now open for students from Nursery up to Class X.

1.Minimum Age Requirements

"The school follows minimum age guidelines to ensure each child is physically and mentally prepared for the class they are seeking admission to. Parents are welcome to contact the school office for any clarification."

Note:- A child must be at least 3 years old by 1st April 2026 to enroll in Nursery.

2.Completed Application for Admission

Admission is only considered after submitting a completed application form and paying the registration and processing fee.

3.Documents to be submitted

Parents must submit the following documents (self-attested copies along with originals for verification):

  • Child's birth certificate (issued by Municipal Corporation)
  • Passport-size photographs of the child and both parents
  • Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC) if applicable
  • Original bonafide certificate
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous school for admission to Class II and above
  • Last academic session's report card and current semester report (if mid-term admission)
  • Medical fitness certificate issued by a certified doctor (MBBS/ MD)
  • Aadhar card copies of child and parents (optional)
  • PAN card / driving license / passport copies of parents for signature verification
  • Relevant legal documents in case of single parent, custody, or death of a parent

Note:- Missing any required document may invalidate the admission application. Originals must be presented for verification. All photocopies will be retained by the school.

4. Admission Procedure

  • Carefully review all instructions for the registration process.
  • Keep the child's photograph ready in both digital (.JPG) and printed passport-size formats.
  • Gather all necessary documents.
  • Fill out the admission form, ensuring all required fields are completed.
  • Pay the registration and processing fees online or at the school using debit/credit card or demand draft (DD).
  • Once approved, fees must be paid within 3 days.
