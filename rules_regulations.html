Rules & Regulations

Learning starts at home, and the school serves as an extension of the foundation laid during a child's early years. At R.A. Bhageria Educational Academy, we place the child at the heart of a collaborative learning triangle consisting of parents, teachers, and the school.

Each of these three pillars plays a vital role in the child's development, and all must work in harmony to ensure the child's continuous growth and success.

(A) Admissions & Enrolment Guidelines

(I) Application Submission

Parents or guardians must submit a completed application form along with all necessary documents to the school office.

Incomplete or incorrect submissions may result in admission being canceled.

(II) Required Documents

Recent passport-sized photo of the student, parents or guardian.

Birth Certificate, Medical Certificate Aadhar Card(student, parents/ guardian)

Previous School Leaving Certificate & School Report Card (if applicable)

Caste & Income Certificates, Migration Certificate, Address Proof, Bank Details

Fee Policy

Fees must be paid on time as per schedule.

Discipline

Strict discipline is maintained at all times.

Uniform

Clean and complete uniform is compulsory.
