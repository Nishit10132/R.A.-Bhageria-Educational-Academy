“Service Before Self”

Our motto, “Service Before Self”, is the cornerstone of the values we nurture at R.A. Bhageria Educational Academy. It teaches our students to put the needs of others above their own, fostering compassion, humility, and responsibility.

What It Means

  • Putting Others First: Recognizing and responding to the needs of others with empathy and kindness.
  • Building Responsible Citizens: Encouraging students to act ethically and contribute positively to society.
  • Developing a Spirit of Giving: Instilling habits of generosity, from small acts of help to larger community service.
  • Strengthening Character & Integrity: Helping students grow in discipline, accountability, and moral courage.
  • Fostering Unity & Harmony: Encouraging collaboration, mutual respect, and social cohesion through service.

Our Commitment

At R.A. Bhageria Educational Academy, this motto guides all aspects of learning — from classroom activities to community service initiatives. By embracing “Service Before Self”, we aim to shape students into compassionate leaders who act with integrity and purpose.
