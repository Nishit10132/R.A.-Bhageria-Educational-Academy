Ms. Nutan Agrawal

Principal

“It is with great pleasure and pride that I extend my warmest greetings to each one of you. At R.A. Bhageria Educational Academy, we believe that the holistic development of every child is essential to face and overcome the challenges of life.”

Our vision is to shape future generations who are compassionate, caring, confident, and guided by values that make them responsible citizens. As the Principal, my goal is not only to nurture, train, and monitor every child so that their potential is fully realized, but also to ensure that no talent remains untouched. Education is a collaborative journey, and together as parents, teachers, and mentors, we share the responsibility of shaping young minds.

With the high expectations of parents and the dedication of our faculty, we strive to prepare students who can contribute meaningfully to society. Today, the role of a school extends far beyond academic excellence. It is also about motivating and empowering students to become lifelong learners and achievers. My team tirelessly and relentlessly provides an atmosphere for multifaceted development, where students can grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.

Parents remain the strongest guiding force in molding their children, and their consistent support empowers us to do more and achieve greater heights.”
